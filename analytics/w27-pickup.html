Method. DSP averages: SFO01 & SMF01 computed on peak day 07/04/2026 (SFO01 total 58,241 — only day over 55k; SMF01 total 16,689 — highest day); avg = received quantity ÷ active routes that day; SFO-ASP excluded (no 07/04 volume). Other 6 stations: daily DSP total ÷ active routes, averaged over 06/29–07/05. Outlier = route exceeding 240 pkgs avg per working day. 07/06 excluded (partial day).
Estimated daily avg/driver/day. Daily route counts were not exported with this dataset, so active routes per day are implied: week-basis stations use (week total ÷ weekly avg) ÷ 7; peak-day stations use peak-day total ÷ peak-day avg. This assumes a constant daily route count — treat the daily avg series as directional. To convert it to actuals, re-export with daily active route counts per DSP.
