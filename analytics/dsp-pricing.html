Analysis summary

Avg price per DSP

Bars = volume-weighted avg $/pkg for each DSP. Dashed line = selection average. Hover for volume served.

DSP × station price matrix

Volume-weighted avg $/pkg per DSP in each station where it operates; darker green = higher price. Overall = station values combined by each station's pkg-volume share of the NC region (normalized over the DSP's stations; hover a cell for the weight). Vol = daily pkg volume attributed to the DSP.
Method. Chart & KPIs: for each DSP, avg price = Σ(ZIP price × DSP volume in that ZIP) ÷ Σ(DSP volume), over priced ZIPs only. Matrix: each cell is the DSP's volume-weighted avg within that station; a DSP's Overall value combines its station cells weighted by each station's pkg-volume share of the NC region, normalized over the stations it operates in (hover a cell for weights); the Station avg row is the station's volume-weighted avg over all priced ZIPs, and the region Overall is Σ(station avg × station volume share).
